OUR MENU

JOLLOF RICE

Our jollof rice is a staple in west africa cuisine. it is made from rice, tomatoes, peppers, and other seasonings. The dish is cooked in one pot. it is typically made with long-grain rice which could be either basmati or the locally sourced rice and can also be cooked with other vegetables and its preparation methods vary across different region

EGUSI SOUP

Our creamy nutty egusi soup is a staple in homes and bukas or street food stalls, across nigeria and in many parts of west africa. The soup takes its name from egusi, the seeds that both thicken and flavors it. Egusi soup typically features meat(such as cow skin, beef, smoked poultry, goat, or offal) and seafood(smoked dried fish or stockfish).

OKRO SOUP

Our Okro soup is a staple in west africa, including Nigeria, Ghana and Cameroon. Our okro soup is deliciously satisfying, nutritious and loaded with proteins and healthy fat. It is made with okro vegetables cooked in a delicious mixture of palm oil, shrimp, goat meat pounded yam, starch and wheat, and other varietties of swallow not mentioned.
